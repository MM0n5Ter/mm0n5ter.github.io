Zhuohao Zhang

My name is Zhuohao Zhang(张倬豪), also named as MM0n5Ter. I am currently a graduate student majoring in Security Informatics in (Johns Hopkins University). I received my Bachelor's degree majoring in Information and Software Engineering from the University of Electronic Science and Technology of China (UESTC). I am still exploring opportunities for a PhD position.

My research interests lie in the system & software security, software analysis, and static analysis. I have worked on several projects centered around Android security, including TPL detection and version matching of Android apps. See my CV and publications for more details about them.
